**Carlos Varga** Programming Journal This site documents my journey into open source programming starting around the year 2020. Mainly it lets me organize and document whatever I've learned along the way. It is somewhat like a blog but with updated posts, so the posts are not kept in date order, but rather topical order. I organize it as a blog with time-sequenced articles, but also have an input queue for things I consider a possibility to blog about. At some point, I intend to organize certain posts into a more topical order. Thus, I dub it a journal. **Philosophy:** Open source all the way and [free/libre](https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html). From the Operating System to various utilities, the articles herein heavily favor open source solutions. Linux itself is largely unfamiliar to me, so I am learning that too. **Requisites** If you're as new to this as I am, you can follow along in my footsteps and let me make the mistakes ahead of you. If you're already ahead of me, this journal may be relatively worthless to you. That said, the articles below are geared towards the following: * Linux flavor: [Debian](https://www.debian.org/) / Ubuntu / [PureOS](https://www.pureos.net/) / maybe even [Guix](https://guix.gnu.org/) * Editor: [VSCodium](https://vscodium.com/) or VSCode, and maybe [Emacs](https://www.gnu.org/software/emacs/) * Languages: [Bash](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bash_%28Unix_shell%29), [Rust](https://www.rust-lang.org/) and Javascript or maybe [Nim](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=25590962) * Version Control: [Git](https://git-scm.com/) and [GitHub](https://github.com/) * Security: [Yubikey](https://www.yubico.com/) and maybe [Keybase](https://keybase.io/) but [probably not](https://restoreprivacy.com/secure-encrypted-messaging-apps/keybase/) Blog ================================================================= Dec 2020 --------------------------------------------------------------- * [Using SSH with Github](articles/using-ssh-with-github.html) * [Creating a GitHub Website](articles/creating-a-github-website.html) * [Generating a PGP key at the Linux commandline](articles/generating-a-pgp-key-at-the-linux-commandline.html) Unfinished Blog Articles ================================================================= * [Password Management](articles/password-management.html) * https://github.com/gopasspw/gopass * https://github.com/drduh/Purse * http://blog.alltherunning.com/2018/02/12/pass-keybase-multi-gpg.html Raw Input Queue ================================================================= Topics I am reading that may or may not make it into future articles.
